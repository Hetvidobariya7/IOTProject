List of Components

  • Arduino

    An Arduino is a single-board microcontroller that allows you to program electronic devices. It simplifies working with electronics by providing a user-friendly interface and pre-written code libraries.

  • Buzzer

    A buzzer is an electronic device that can produce sound. It's often used to provide audible feedback or warnings in circuits.

  • Jumper Wire

    Jumper wires are flexible wires with connectors at both ends. They are used to connect components on a breadboard or PCB without soldering.

  • LED (Light-Emitting Diode)

    An LED is a small electronic component that emits light when current flows through it. LEDs come in various colors and are used for indication, illumination, and decoration in circuits.

  • PIR Sensor (Passive Infrared Sensor)

    A PIR sensor detects changes in infrared radiation, which is emitted by all objects with a temperature above absolute zero. This makes it useful for motion detection applications.

  • ESP8266

    The ESP8266 is a low-cost, Wi-Fi enabled microchip that can be used to add wireless connectivity to your projects. It allows your devices to connect to the internet and communicate with other devices over Wi-Fi.
